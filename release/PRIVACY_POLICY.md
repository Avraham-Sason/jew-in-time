# Privacy Policy — Kosher Jew

Effective date: 2026-04-23

`Kosher Jew` is a local-first mitzvot reminder app. The app is designed to work without creating an account and without requiring a backend for core functionality.

## Data We Process

The app may process:

- Approximate or precise location, if you allow it, to calculate halachic times.
- Notification permission status, to schedule local reminders.
- Local preferences such as nusach, language, theme, enabled mitzvot, and reminder configuration.
- Local completion history for mitzvot you mark as done.

## Where Data Is Stored

Core app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. By default, mitzvot settings, reminders, and completion history are not sent to a remote server.

## Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated from your local settings and halachic-time calculations.

## Location

If you grant location access, the app uses location data to calculate zmanim for your current area. If location access is denied, you can choose a city manually.

## Sharing

The app does not sell your personal data.

The current build does not require account signup and does not sync mitzvot data to a server by default.

## Optional Diagnostics

Future production builds may include optional crash reporting such as Sentry. If enabled, crash reports may include technical context such as app version, device model, operating system version, route, and error stack trace. Sensitive content should not be intentionally included in crash metadata.

## Your Choices

- You can deny location permission and use a manual city instead.
- You can deny notifications and continue using the app without alerts.
- You can reset app data by clearing local app storage or uninstalling the app.

## Children

The app is not directed specifically to children under 13.

## Contact

For privacy questions, publish the support contact email and website URL that will be used for the store listing before release.
